About me

I am a postdoctoral research associate in the Advanced Controls Research Laboratory and the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), hosted by Prof. Naira Hovakimyan and Prof. Lui Sha. From Oct. 2018 to Jan. 2019, I was a visiting researcher in the Advanced Controls Research Laboratory and the Coordinated Science Laboratory at UIUC, hosted by Prof. Naira Hovakimyan. I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the State University of New York at Binghamton in Sept. 2019, co-advised by Prof. Ziang Zhang and Prof. Emrah Akyol. From Jan. 2014 to Mar. 2015, I studied traffic networks at Oregon State University, under the supervision of Prof. Haizhong Wang.
